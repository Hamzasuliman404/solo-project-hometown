Visit Khartoum

Enjoy culture and sports in this vibrant district close to the center of Khartoum.

Top three activities to do at Khartoum

Attend a show or a concert

The Khartoum Theatre is a great place to spend your night out.

Go for a piknic in the local park

The Khartoum Park is perfect for piknic, getting some tan, or just chilling out.

Play sports at the Khartoum field

Play volleyball, football, basket ball or lift some weights.
